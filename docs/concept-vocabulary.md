# Concept Vocabulary

The neutral legal concepts used to tag provisions in the derived layer. Each describes what a provision is *about*; the vocabulary is doctrine-agnostic. Tagging is derived, unofficial, and traceable to each authoritative text (see `docs/design/06-two-layer-separation.md`).

- **`freedom_of_exploration_and_use`** — Freedom to explore and use outer space by all States; province/benefit of all; freedom of scientific investigation.
- **`non_appropriation`** — Outer space and celestial bodies not subject to national appropriation by sovereignty, use, occupation or other means.
- **`peaceful_use_and_non_militarization`** — Peaceful purposes; prohibition of WMD/weapons; no military bases; no intentional harmful acts.
- **`state_responsibility_and_supervision`** — International responsibility of States for national activities, including authorization and continuing supervision of non-governmental entities.
- **`jurisdiction_and_control`** — Retention of jurisdiction and control over space objects and personnel; ownership of objects.
- **`registration`** — Registration of space objects in national and UN registries; furnishing of identifying information.
- **`liability_and_damage`** — Responsibility/liability for damage caused by space objects; compensation; claims procedures.
- **`rescue_and_return`** — Rescue and return of astronauts/personnel in distress; assistance.
- **`abandonment_and_salvage`** — Ownership, recovery and return of space objects (and parts) found beyond the launching State's limits.
- **`resource_rights`** — Rights over natural resources of celestial bodies; exploitation; common-heritage regime; samples.
- **`environmental_protection_and_debris`** — Protection of the space and Earth environment; harmful contamination; space debris mitigation; long-term sustainability.
- **`international_cooperation_and_transparency`** — International cooperation; information-sharing; notification; consultation; benefit-sharing; capacity-building; openness.
- **`dispute_settlement_and_consultation`** — Consultation and settlement of disputes; claims commissions; arbitration.
- **`safety_of_space_operations`** — Safety of operations; conjunction assessment; collision avoidance; re-entry safety; nuclear-power-source and operational safety.
