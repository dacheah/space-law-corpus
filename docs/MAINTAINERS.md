# Maintainer's Guide

**Status:** placeholder — to be written in full in Phase G (transferability & succession).

The goal of this guide: a competent stranger could take over the corpus from this document alone. It will cover:

- The one principle (provenance and version integrity override convenience) and why.
- The two-layer wall and how it is enforced.
- How to ingest a new authoritative document (step by step, with the hashing and metadata rules).
- How versioning works (never overwrite; new dated versions).
- The annual review ritual (aligned to the COPUOS Legal Subcommittee session) and how to trigger it early.
- How the automated monitoring works and what to do when it flags a change.
- Licensing rules per source type.
- How to find things and how the identifiers work.

A note on succession: finding a permanent institutional home for the corpus is a **future milestone, not a present dependency.** Everything is kept in open, portable, well-documented formats precisely so that transfer is easy when the time comes.
